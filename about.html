About

I'm one with an adventurous spirit that enjoys the little things in life, like a plane flying over the bright clouds or just the sound of rain and how it softens the outlines of things.

I walk hand in hand with my camera ever since I can remember. As I was being pulled out by sheer curiosity about this art, I would think to myself that each and every moment is unrepeatable and inimitable, thus I would always have the urge to capture moments, even the slightest details.

I soon discovered the passion for sharing knowledge because when you transmit what you know, you learn even more with people you're surrounded with. This drives us to think further about a photograph and how to communicate.
